Production cycle
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Ordered sequence of couples of workshops and production timings.

Properties:

- Associated to a unique part

Use Cases:

-   Operators Optimization
-   Machines Estimate
-   Operators Assignment
-   PMIM Formation
-   Sequencing
-   Takt Time 
-   From-to Chart Formation
-   Assignment of products/parts to clusters
